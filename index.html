Our Story

We are the Zeek family—Ashtyn, Dave, Emmalee, Bella, and Ben. Deeply rooted right here in Deale, Maryland, our family is built on a foundation of service, dedication, and home. Dave proudly serves on active duty as an Army Sergeant Major, while Ashtyn pours her heart into bringing comfort and peace of mind to families across our community.

Cleaning isn't just a business for us; it’s a family tradition. As a second-generation house cleaner, Ashtyn grew up learning that a clean home isn't just about a spotless surface—it’s about creating a sanctuary where families can truly thrive, rest, and make memories.

We know how precious time with your loved ones is. That’s why we treat your home with the exact same care, respect, and devotion we give to our own. When you work with us, you aren't just hiring a service—you are welcoming neighbors who care.
