Routine Cleaning Services

Designed to maintain a clean, comfortable, and welcoming home through recurring weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly service.

Area Tasks Included (not limited to)
Kitchen Wet-wipe countertops, backsplash, and appliance exteriors. Clean and sanitize sink and stovetop. Clean light switches and door handles. Dust reachable window trim, door trim, and baseboards as needed. Vacuum and mop floors. Empty trash.
Bathrooms Wet-wipe and sanitize sinks, countertops, fixtures, and cabinet fronts. Clean mirrors, showers, and tubs. Clean light switches and door handles. Dust reachable window trim, door trim, and baseboards as needed. Vacuum and mop floors. Clean and sanitize toilets. Empty trash.
Bedrooms Wet-wipe reachable furniture surfaces, décor, and shelving as needed. Clean light switches and door handles. Dust reachable window trim, door trim, and baseboards as needed. Make beds (if linens are provided). Vacuum or mop floors. Empty trash.
Common Areas Wet-wipe reachable surfaces, décor, and shelving as needed. Clean glass surfaces, light switches, and door handles. Dust reachable window trim, door trim, and baseboards as needed. Vacuum carpets and rugs. Mop hard floors.

Deep Cleaning Services

A comprehensive, top-to-bottom clean that restores every corner to pristine condition. Includes everything in Routine Cleaning, plus:

Area Additional Deep Cleaning Tasks
Kitchen Hand-wash baseboards and detailed wipe of window sills/tracks. Clean inside oven, inside microwave, and spot-clean inside refrigerator. Detailed scrubbing of grout lines and deep cleaning of range hood. Wipe down interior of empty cabinets and drawers.
Bathrooms Hand-wash baseboards. Intensive scrubbing and descaling of shower walls, tubs, and glass doors to remove buildup. Detailed scrubbing of tile grout. Wipe down interior of empty vanity cabinets and drawers.
Bedrooms & Living Areas Hand-wash baseboards and detailed wipe of window sills/tracks. Move light furniture to vacuum/mop underneath. Detail dusting of ceiling fans, AC vents, and blinds. Wipe down all doors and door frames. Vacuum upholstery.
